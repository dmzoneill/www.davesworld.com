The Hanson Board Game

How to play:
(1) Put a check in one of the boxes
(2) The number it says in the box that pops up is the number of spaces you move in the circles.
(3) If a box pops up on one of the circles do what it says.
(4) This game can be played with one or two players
(5) If you get more than enough to finish the game then you have to stay where you are until you get the exact or less number to finish.

Your goal is to get past security and to go on stage with Hanson at the finish.
